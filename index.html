Maroni

A galactic ambition

Moroni is a disruptive blockchain aiming at replacing the current centralized market infrastrucuture for commodities. We provide you with crypto tokens representing a certain amount of a given commodity, in exchange for crypotocurrencies such as bitcoin or ethereum. This is thought to remove the geopolitical factor of the commodity market to ensure lower prices and more resilient supply chains. We also take care of the logisitics.
